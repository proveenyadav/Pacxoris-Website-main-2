Our Mission

AI-Powered Radiology Infrastructure Built for India Powered by IQ-Line

India's diagnostic sector is growing faster than any infrastructure is keeping up with. CT and MRI volumes have multiplied 10–12 times. Yet the average radiologist still spends nearly two-thirds of their working day on administrative tasks worklists, reporting paperwork, inter-department coordination and less than a third actually reading scans.

The tools built for radiology departments were designed for a different era fragmented, inflexible, and disconnected from the rest of the hospital. Radiologists toggled between three different systems just to complete one report. Patients waited. Administrators guessed. Abnormalities were missed under the pressure of volume.

PACXORIS is our answer to that. An AI-powered radiology platform built from the ground up to unify imaging operations, reduce administrative burden, and give every person in the workflow radiologist, technician, administrator, patient exactly what they need.

Mission

Make expert radiology accessible to every patient, at every facility, at any scale.

Radiology expertise should not be a geographic privilege. PACXORIS is building the infrastructure that lets hospitals in underserved regions access on-demand tele-radiology, lets radiologists work at their highest cognitive capacity, and lets patients receive their results accurately and quickly without having to physically queue.

Vision

A future where no diagnosis is delayed by infrastructure.

We envision a healthcare ecosystem where AI handles the routine, humans handle the judgment, and the entire radiology pipeline from scan to report to patient operates without friction. PACXORIS is the platform that makes that possible not as a pilot, but at national scale.

The Future of Radiology is Intelligent.

PACXORIS is building the operational layer that enables radiology teams to deliver faster diagnoses, better patient outcomes and sustainable growth.
